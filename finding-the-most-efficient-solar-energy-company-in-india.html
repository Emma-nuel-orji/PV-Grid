Finding the Most Efficient Solar Energy Company in India

  1. Home
  2. Blog
Solar energy is one of the most promising sources of renewable energy in the world. It is clean, abundant, and affordable. India, being a tropical country, has a huge potential for harnessing solar power. According to the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE), India has set a target of installing 100 GW of solar power capacity by 2022¹. However, to achieve this goal, India needs to have a robust solar manufacturing industry that can produce high-quality and efficient solar panels.

In this blog, we will introduce you to Novasys Greenergy, one of the best solar energy Company in India, and how to choose the best solar panel company in India for your home.


Who is Novasys Greenergy?


Novasys Greenergy is one of the leading solar energy Company in India, with a manufacturing capacity of 1.5 GW. It produces high-efficiency solar modules using advanced technology and quality materials. It offers a range of products, such as the Orion Series, the Alpha Plus Series, the Pulsar Series, the Rigel Plus Series, and the Vega Series, with efficiency ranging from 16% to 21%. It also provides EPC services, project development, rooftop solutions, solar water pumps, and an Independent Power Producer. It has a 10-year product and 25-year performance warranty on its solar modules.

Novasys Green is a part of the Novasys Group, which has been in the solar industry since 2004. It is headquartered in Nagpur, Maharashtra, and has a presence in 18 states across India. It has a team of over 500 professionals, including engineers, technicians, and consultants, who are dedicated to delivering the best solar solutions to its customers. It has a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility, equipped with the latest machines and equipment sourced from the best in class producers. It has a quality certification from the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS), the International Electrotechnical Commission (IEC), and the MNRE.

Novasys Greenergy is committed to building a sustainable future by harnessing the power of solar panels. It is focused on customer loyalty with an array of items, services, and strong after-sales support with a fantastic plan of system, improvement, and manufacturing skills.

What are the features and benefits of Novasys Greenergy's solar panels?


Novasys Greenergy's solar panels are designed to meet the diverse needs and preferences of its customers. Here are some of the features and benefits of its solar panels:


  • The Orion Series: The Orion Series is a polycrystalline solar module that has a high conversion efficiency and a positive power output tolerance. It has an excellent performance in low light and low irradiance conditions. It is 100% EL inspected to ensure micro crack free modules. It is ideal for residential, commercial, industrial, and agricultural applications.

  • The Alpha Plus Series: The Alpha Plus Series is a monocrystalline solar module that uses the latest technology of MBB, M10 half cut cells, providing higher output and high reliability. It has a better performance under low light and high temperature conditions. It has a lower temperature coefficient and a higher fill factor. It is ideal for commercial, residential, industrial, and institutional projects.

  • The Pulsar Series: The Pulsar Series is a polycrystalline solar module that is designed with the new generation PERC technology. It has a higher cell efficiency than conventional polycrystalline cells. It has more power output per square meter and an outstanding low light and longer wavelength performance. It is ideal for residential, commercial, industrial, and agricultural applications.

  • The Rigel Plus Series: The Rigel Plus Series is a monocrystalline solar module that has up to 30% increase in efficiency. It has a better performance under low light and high temperature conditions. It has a very high power output per square meter and a higher fill factor. It is ideal for commercial, residential, industrial, and institutional projects.

  • The Vega Series: The Vega Series is a bifacial solar module that can generate power from both the front and the back sides of the module. It has a higher energy yield and a lower levelized cost of electricity. It has a better performance under low light and high temperature conditions. It has a lower temperature coefficient and a higher fill factor. It is ideal for large-scale utility projects.

How to choose the best solar panel company in India for your home?


Choosing the best solar panel company in India for your home depends on your specific needs and preferences. However, some general tips that you can follow are:


  • Compare the features and prices of different solar panel models and brands and select the one that suits your budget and requirements.

  • Check the reviews and ratings of the solar panel company and its products online and offline and look for customer feedback and testimonials.

  • Verify the warranty and certification of the solar panel company and its products and ensure that they are valid and authentic.

  • Look for the after-sales service and support of the solar panel company and its products and ensure that they are prompt and responsive.

  • Consult a solar expert or a solar installer and get their advice and guidance on the best solar panel company and its products for your home.
